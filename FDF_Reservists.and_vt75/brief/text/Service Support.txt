<font color='#FFA500' size='18'>Service Support</font>
<br/>
<br/>1. Hotel squad
<br/>
<br/>Hotel squad is responsible for logistics and will keep the platoons supplied with ammunition and medical supplies, and ferry later reinforcment units to the front line. 
<br/>It will also handle CASEVAC and MEDEVAC to HQ.
<br/>
<br/><font color='#99cc00'>Medical</font>
<br/>
<br/>Casualties are to be stabilized in situ and then brought back to Platoon headquarters if further medical treatment is required; (They will take longer than 2-3 minutes to wake up or will not wake up at all without the platoon medic’s intervention.)
<br/>
<br/>ACE Basic. Battle Casualty Replacements (BCR) as per SOP (Respawn at Waiting Area at Battalion HQ for wave respawn, deployment to Assembly Area marker location once respawn countdown completes.)
