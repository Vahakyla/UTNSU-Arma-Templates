<font color='#FFA500' size='18'>Execution</font>
<br/>
<br/><font color='#99cc00'>Commander's Intent</font>
<br/>
<br/>Commander’s intent is to establish a main life of defense at the marked location on the highway and along the established fortifications running north and south from it (Fig. 2).
<br/>At this location, the company’s AT units will defend the road, supported by an artillery officer to direct indirect fire.
<br/>
<br/>The MG units will spread out along the fortifications in the woods to maximize covered area and halt enemy infantry advances, aided by the remaining artillery officers.
<br/>One MG unit should stay close to the road in case of dismounting enemy infantry.
<br/>
<br/>Rifle units will spread out along the fortifications and the road between them to defend as necessary.
<br/>
<br/>Prior to engagement, in addition to the existing minefield north of the road, AT mines will be laid on the road and AP mines in the woods east of the fortifications.
<br/>No friendly advance will be made into this area.
<br/>
<br/>Should this first line of defence be breached, two more defensible locations are available on the hills along the road west (Fig. 3, Fig. 4).
<br/>Time permitting, these will be fortified in advance. If this is not possible, existing buildings and natural features may be used.
<br/>
<br/>Should the third line be breached, all capable units will execute a fighting retreat and maximize enemy attrition while making their way back to battalion HQ to the west.
<br/>
<br/><font color='#99cc00'>Scheme of Maneuvers</font>
<br/>
<br/>Alpha platoon will assume responsibility for the section south of the road. 
<br/>
<br/>Charlie platoon will assume responsibility for the section north of the road. 
<br/>
<br/>Bravo platoon will support with MG and medical units as required.
<br/>Withdrawals will not be executed without coordination with the other platoons and thus require COs approval.
<br/>
<br/>Prior to mission start, all available units will begin laying of mines east of the first line of defense, and construction of defensive structures first on the road at the first line of defense, progressing to second and
<br/>third as time permits. At mission start, all units will rejoin their platoons at the first line of defense.
<br/>
<br/><font color='#99cc00'>Fires</font>
<br/>
<br/>Platoons will engage enemy forces on sight. The spaces ahead of the first line of defense and between lines should be pre-sighted and marked for indirect fire support.
<br/>
<br/>Papa squad will deploy its weapons next to the road at the defense lines at their own discretion with the goal of maximizing their ability to engage heavy enemy forces on the road.
