<font color='#FFA500' size='18'>Execution</font>
<br/>
<br/><font color='#99cc00'>Commander's Intent</font>
<br/>
<br/>Commander’s intent is to establish a layered defence along the Northern and Southern sides of highway 941 divided into 3 ‘phaselines’;
<br/>
<br/>1. A series of light pickets held in fireteam strength to engage and half initial enemy motorized reconnaissance troops before at a distance from proper defences.
<br/>
<br/>2. Mutually supporting strongpoints manned in squad strength behind the picket line to provide supporting fire to the 1st line and to act as the initial line of resistance against the main enemy effort.
<br/>
<br/>3. A heavily fortified final line of resistance defended in platoon strength providing supporting fire to the second line.
<br/>
<br/>Should the third line be breached, whatever units are combat capable will either continue a fighting retreat along the main highway or disperse into the forests and independently make their way to the rally point at Battalion headquarters to the West. 
<br/>Reference sketch for rough visual representation of intended defensive plan.
<br/>
<br/><font color='#99cc00'>Scheme of Maneuvers</font>
<br/>
<br/>Alpha Platoon will assume responsibility for the Northern section of the road with Charlie assuming responsibility for the Southern section of the road. Bravo platoon will act as a reserve platoon.
<br/>
<br/>Alpha and Charlie platoon will each designate up to two squads to hold the strongpoints at phaseline 2 and one of those squads will designate a fireteam to hold the forward outpost. Troops will be pulled back from the first and second phaselines at the 
<br/>Platoon leader’s discretion or upon the Company CO’s instruction, any withdrawals or significant movements outside of fortifications should be coordinated with other squads within the platoon.
<br/>
<br/><font color='#99cc00'>Fires</font>
<br/>
<br/>Platoons will exercise fire discipline and coordinate in order to engage enemy forces decisively and quickly. The spaces ahead of the outpost line and between each phaseline should be marked as target reference points and pre-sighted for battalion mortars 
<br/>and brigade artillery. The former will be distributed to platoon-level forward observers while the latter will be reserved for the Company Artillery officer.
<br/>
<br/>The anti tank detachment will deploy its weapons and crews at its own discretion in the positions that most effectively balance survivability and ability to engage mechanized and motorized targets coming down the road.
<br/>
<br/>Overall Company ROE is Orange, Hostile forces are not to be engaged until they cross the truce line or open fire or Company CO affirms otherwise.
