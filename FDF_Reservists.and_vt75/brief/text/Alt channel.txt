<font color='#FFA500' size='18'>Setting up an alternate TFAR channel</font>
<br/>
<br/>1) Go to a channel you wont use (usually channel 9) 
<br/>2) Set its frequency (Command, other lead elements, etc) 
<br/>3) Set the channel as your Alternative Channel (arrow keys). 
<br/>4) Set the channel to one of your ears (arrow keys). 
<br/>5) Go back to your Teams Channel and set that to your other ear 
<br/>6) Now you listen to both channels and talk to your team with Caps Lock(CH1) , talk to Command(Alt Channel) with T
